## **1\. Brainstorming**

### **Commençons par le brainstorming.**

Si vous utilisez ce prompt dans Bard, vous avez la réponse suivante :

**Prompt :** *You are a seo and behavior psychology expert. Your task is to help the user find domain name ideas in the game board with focus on strategy. Brainstorm 10 domain ideas :*

⚠️ Bard (version standard) refuse de répondre avec le message : *"I'm a language model and don't have the capacity to help with that."*

Eh oui, vous avez bien lu, Bard refuse de le faire. Pour votre information, ChatGPT répond sans souci.

Si on veut profiter des technologies Google, il faut donc passer par notre configuration de Bard dans **Vertex AI**. (Chapitres 5 et 7\)

### **Résultat via Vertex AI (Bard v2)**

Après configuration dans Vertex AI, voici la conversation :

**USER :** You are a seo and behavior psychology expert. Your task is to help the user find domain name ideas in the gameboard with focus on strategy. Brainstorm 10 domain ideas :

**AI :**

1. StrategyGame.com  
2. StrategyGames.com  
3. StrategyGamesOnline.com  
4. StrategyGameStrategy.com  
5. StrategyGameTips.com  
6. StrategyGameTutorials.com  
7. StrategyGameForums.com  
8. StrategyGameDownloads.com  
9. StrategyGameReviews.com  
10. StrategyGameArticles.com

**USER :** I picked the third. The website is about game board with focus on strategy.

**AI :** I think that's a great choice\! StrategyGamesOnline.com is a very descriptive and informative domain name that will help users find your website easily. It also has a nice ring to it, which is important for branding purposes.

## **2\. Topical Map**

**Prompt :** *Can you use your expertise and create a SEO Topical map for all the topics we should cover in this niche?*

Ici, nous pouvons retrouver un plan très utile à déployer pour son contenu, les idées suggérées sont excellentes.

**USER :** Can you use your expertise and create a SEO Topical map for all the topics we should cover in this niche?

**AI :** Sure, here is a list of topics that you could cover in your website:

* How to play strategy board games  
* Different types of strategy board games  
* Strategies for winning strategy board games  
* Tips for beginners to strategy board games  
* Reviews of popular strategy board games  
* Where to buy strategy board games  
* Events and tournaments for strategy board games  
* Online resources for strategy board games  
* History of strategy board games  
* Culture and society of strategy board games

These are just a few ideas to get you started. You can also think of your own topics that are relevant to your website and audience.
